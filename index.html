Jane Doe

Watch the Video

Jane Doe writes songs to honor a true nostalgia for long gone times, with the themes ranging from fighting old demons to dreams of new love, and the adventures that lie beyond the sun setting on the horizon. Her soft voice in its sweetness and fragility is complemented by metallic sounds of electric guitars and smooth rhythms conveying both atmosphere and the singer's own brutally honest emotional perspective, invoking stylistic comparisons to contemporaries such as Angel Olsen or Julien Baker.  While her inspirations are many and span a wide range of genres and decades, the most notable ones would include songwriting legends like Bob Dylan or Joni Mitchell, newer influences like Phoebe Bridgers or Lucy Dacus, and classic Rock’n’Roll and Jazz sounds. Born and raised in Germany, her first studio-recorded song „Bonnie“ debuts on Dec 17th, 2021.
